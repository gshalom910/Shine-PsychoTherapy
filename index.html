About Us

About Us

Welcome to SHINE, PsychoTherapy

We are dedicated to providing specialized psychotherapy for women with disabilities. Based in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia, we are committed to offering a safe, inclusive, and empowering environment for women to seek the care and support they deserve.

At SHINE, we understand the unique challenges faced by women with disabilities and believe in the power of mental well-being to transform lives. Our team of experienced and compassionate therapists is trained to work with diverse conditions and tailored approaches, ensuring that each woman receives personalized care that meets her specific needs.
