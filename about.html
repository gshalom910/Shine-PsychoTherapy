About Us

About Us

Welcome to SHINE, PsychoTherapy

We are dedicated to providing specialized psychotherapy for women with disabilities. Based in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia, we are committed to offering a safe, inclusive, and empowering environment for women to seek the care and support they deserve.

At SHINE, we understand the unique challenges faced by women with disabilities and believe in the power of mental well-being to transform lives. Our team of experienced and compassionate therapists is trained to work with diverse conditions and tailored approaches, ensuring that each woman receives personalized care that meets her specific needs.

Vission

  • To be the leading global platform for mental health support, helping women with disabilities achieve emotional well-being and thrive.
  • To build a world where mental health care is accessible from any geographical location, stigma-free, and equitable for women with disabilities.
  • To revolutionize psychological care through tech-driven solutions, promoting independence and resilience for women with disabilities.
  • To be a trusted partner in supporting the mental health of women with disabilities, promoting holistic well-being worldwide.
Mission

Our mission is to break barriers in mental health care across Ethiopia by providing accessible and inclusive online therapy that empowers women with disabilities. Through compassionate support and innovative technology, we celebrate Ethiopia’s diversity and promote resilience, inclusiveness, and equality.

Our Values
Inclusivity

Creating a welcoming environment that values diversity and amplifies the voices of women with disabilities.

Empathy

Offering care that understands and respects the unique experiences of every individual, while providing compassionate, client-centered support.

Quality
